More about the story behind the brand.

Meghan began her journey rebranding a beloved, locally-owned small business in Brevard County, Florida, and has since expanded her portfolio to include multiple hospitality brands throughout Florida, from the Space Coast to the Gulf Coast. Known for her hands-on approach, Meghan is more than just a content creator; she is the backbone of her brand. From photographing dishes and capturing behind-the-scenes content to designing custom logos, menus, and branded materials, she ensures every visual element reflects the heart of each brand.

Using professional-grade equipment and staying ahead of digital trends, Meghan helps brands connect with their audiences in engaging ways. Her focus is on building organic visibility with no shortcuts, just thoughtful strategy and standout visuals that are custom to each brand she works with.

She is currently pursuing her Bachelor’s degree in Communication Arts with a minor in Marketing at Florida International University, combining academic insight with real-world experience.

If you are looking to transform your brand's online presence with someone who understands the industry, values collaboration, and brings fresh, creative ideas to the table, Meghan Knaple is ready to bring your brand to life.
